- **Commit:** `2f215ac`

### Plan-author calibration (drift-palette exemption)

The plan body wrote the test/grep gates strictly (e.g. "no `bg-amber-50` anywhere") but the must-have explicitly scoped the bg-amber-50 ban to "decorative chip on /compare landing page". The two are inconsistent because `components/compare/{DiffNodeList,DiffSummary}` use `bg-amber-50` semantically as the drift-palette `changed` token (per UI-SPEC §Color drift palette). Resolved by exempting `components/compare/*` from the strict gates — codified in both `polish-drift.test.ts` (`isExempt`) and `polish-drift-grep.sh` (`--exclude-dir=compare` scoped to components/, not app/). The /compare page surface (under app/) is still subject to all gates.
