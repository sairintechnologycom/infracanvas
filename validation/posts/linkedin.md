## LinkedIn Format Notes (per D-03, D-05)

- **Short paragraphs:** LinkedIn suppresses long-form walls of text. Each sentence or two on its own line.
- **Video embed:** Attach the demo video directly to the LinkedIn post — do NOT link to an external video URL. LinkedIn natively autoplays video silently on scroll (optimised for D-03).
- **Link in comments:** LinkedIn's algorithm penalises posts with external links in the post body. Put the infracanvas.dev link in the first comment, not in the body. The "Link in comments" line in the post body signals to readers where to find it.
- **No hashtag spam:** 2–3 relevant hashtags maximum (#devops #terraform #infrastructure). More than 5 looks like spam.
- **Timing:** Post during Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM in the target audience timezone (US East or US West Coast, whichever is primary).
